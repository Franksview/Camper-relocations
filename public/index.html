What is Movacamper?

Rental companies constantly need campervans and motorhomes moved between cities — after the summer rush, vehicles pile up in the south and need to go north. Instead of hiring drivers, they let travelers like you drive them for as little as €1 per day.

Movacamper searches five providers in real time so you don't have to check them all yourself. We built this because we kept missing deals scattered across different sites.

Is it really free?

Yes — for you. We earn a small commission when you book through a provider link. You never pay more than you would going directly. Most relocation deals cost between €1–5 per day, and some even include fuel allowances.
