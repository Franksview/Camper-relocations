About Movacamper

Movacamper is a free search engine for campervan and motorhome relocation deals across Europe. We aggregate live deals from five providers — Imoova, Roadsurfer, Indie Campers, Bunk Campers, and Movacar — so you can find them in one place.

How relocation deals work

After peak season, rental companies end up with campervans in the wrong cities. Instead of paying drivers, they offer the vehicles to travellers for as little as €1 per day. You pick up the van in one city, drive it to another within a set number of days, and enjoy a fully equipped campervan along the way — bed, kitchen, insurance included.

Why we built this

We kept missing deals because they were scattered across different provider websites, each with their own search interface. Movacamper was built to solve that: one search, all providers, updated daily.

How we make money

Movacamper is completely free for travellers. When you click through to a provider and book a deal, we may earn a small referral commission. You never pay more than you would going to the provider directly.

Who we are

Movacamper is a small independent project built in Amsterdam, the Netherlands, by a backpacker who couldn't afford rental cars. We're not affiliated with any rental company.
